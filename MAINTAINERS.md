# Maintainers

The following people maintain the **MCP Description Editor** (`mcptoolkit-editor`). They are responsible for
triaging issues, reviewing pull requests, and shipping releases.

| Maintainer | GitHub | Area |
|---|---|---|
| Stève Sfartz | [@stsfartz](https://github.com/stsfartz) | Lead maintainer |

## Becoming a maintainer

Active contributors who have demonstrated sustained, high-quality contributions
to the project may be invited to become maintainers. If you are interested in
helping maintain the project, start by:

1. Triaging issues and reviewing pull requests.
2. Contributing well-scoped, well-tested changes.
3. Engaging with the community in a manner consistent with the
   [Code of Conduct](/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

Existing maintainers will reach out when an invitation is appropriate.
